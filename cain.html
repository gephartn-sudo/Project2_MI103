CAIN

Attributes

The unique thing about CAIN is that there is no attributes, there are only skills. However, these skills are effectively attributes in CAIN. For example, the "Coordination" skill and "Covert" skill could comprise of what is usually known as Dexterity as an attribute. Having a higher level in a skill means you roll more dice when you roll that skill, giving more chances for successes.
