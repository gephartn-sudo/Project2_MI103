Dungeons & Dragons 5th Edition

Attributes

Dungeons and Dragons 5th edition has 6 attributes that range from 0 to 20 (without magic items increasing the limits), which are Strength, Constitution, Dexterity, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma. These determine how good you are innately at specific skills, how easy or difficult it is to affect you through that attribute (saving throws), as well as spellcasting modifiers and hit points and armor class... there's a lot attributes affect in Dungeons and Dragons 5th edition.

Skills

  • Athletics - Based on Strength
  • Acrobatics - Based on Dexterity
  • Sleight of Hand - Based on Dexterity
  • Stealth - Based on Dexterity
  • Arcana - Based on Intelligence
  • History - Based on Intelligence
  • Investigation - Based on Intelligence
  • Nature - Based on Intelligence
  • Religion - Based on Intelligence
  • Animal Handling - Based on Wisdom
  • Insight - Based on Wisdom
  • Medicine - Based on Wisdom
  • Perception - Based on Wisdom
  • Survival - Based on Wisdom
  • Deception - Based on Charisma
  • Intimidation - Based on Charisma
  • Performance - Based on Charisma
  • Persuasion - Based on Charisma
