GURPS

Attributes

GURPS has 4 main attributes: ST, DX, HT, and IQ, which is Strength, Dexterity, Health, and IQ respectively. There are technically no hard limits, however some often used rules would limit how much you can penalize your character and how much stronger than the average 10 a character can be. There are a few secondary attributes which are based off of the main 4 attributes as well: HP (Hit Points) is based off of ST, Per (Perception) is based off of IQ, and your Basic Move (your base movement speed) is based off of your DX added with your HT, then divided by 4. These attributes comprise most of your character.

Skills

There are FAR too many skills in GURPS to list them all off here. However, it is worth noting that most skills have something called a Default, which makes it so that if you don't have a specific skill learnt, you can roll at a penalty to something else (usually an attribute, but sometimes another skill if you have it) instead of being a fish out of the water when it comes to that skill.
